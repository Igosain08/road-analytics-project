Conclusions and Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are made for improving road safety:

  1. Target high-risk hotspots: Focus safety improvements on the identified accident clusters, particularly those with high severity ratings.
  2. Implement time-based interventions: Increase enforcement and safety measures during high-risk time periods identified in the temporal analysis.
  3. Address weather-related risks: Improve road maintenance and warning systems during adverse weather conditions, particularly on high-risk road segments.
  4. Modify high-risk road segments: Consider structural improvements to road segments identified as high-risk by the predictive model.
  5. Conduct further analysis: Collect additional data on vehicle types, driver factors, and exact accident circumstances to improve severity prediction.
